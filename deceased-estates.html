Compassionate Estate Services

Dealing with the financial and tax obligations of a deceased estate can be overwhelming during an already difficult time. Our experienced team provides compassionate, comprehensive support to help executors and beneficiaries navigate the complex requirements of estate administration whilst ensuring full compliance with Australian tax law.

From preparing deceased estate tax returns to calculating capital gains on inherited assets, we handle all aspects of estate taxation with care and precision. Our expertise ensures that all obligations are met efficiently, allowing families to focus on what matters most during this challenging period.

Complete Estate Services

Deceased Estate Tax Returns

Preparation and lodgement of final tax returns and estate tax returns with all required schedules and disclosures.

Capital Gains Calculations

Accurate calculation of capital gains tax on asset disposals including application of available exemptions and concessions.

Cost Base Valuations

Professional asset valuations and cost base calculations for inherited properties, shares, and other investments.

ATO Liaison

Professional representation and communication with the Australian Taxation Office on behalf of the estate and beneficiaries.
